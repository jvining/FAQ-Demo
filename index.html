What is NGCX? 

In six short years, NGCX (Next Generation Customer Experience)has grown from a small CX professional meetup to one of the largest CX conferences in America. Launched in 2011, NGCX brings together senior level customer experience executives across all industries to discuss the latest customer experience strategies across all channels and touch points. If you want to develop a cutting-edge CX strategy or benefit from focused networking, we invite you to attend.

Where does NGCX take place? 

NGXC 2016 is taking place at the Park Hyatt Aviara Resort in Carlsbad, California.

What does my NGCX ticket entitle me to? 

A ticket to NGCX grants you entry to the conference as well as admission to all of our networking events. Additionally, your ticket includes breakfast and lunch on conference days. For any ticket enquires email customerexperience@wbresearch.com.

I can’t attend NGCX anymore. Can I get a refund? 

Cancelations, received in writing no less than eight days prior to the conference, will received a 90% credit to be used at another WBR conference. An administration fee of 10% of the contract fee will be retained by WBR for all permitted cancellations. No credit will be issued for any cancellations occurring within seven (7) days (inclusive) of the conference.
